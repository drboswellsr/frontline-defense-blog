Post Two: Responsive Design Matters

Responsive design ensures your website looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops. In this project, the blog posts stack vertically on small screens and sit side-by-side on larger screens.

The contact form is also designed to scale smoothly across devices, with focus and hover transitions to improve usability and accessibility.
